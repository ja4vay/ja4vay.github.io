Where to Stay

Positano

Book a stay at La Sirenuse Positano. This stylish 58 room hotel is conveniently located in Positano. The hotel opened in 1952 and was originally a vacation house for the Machesis siblings. La Sirenuse's charm can be contributed to the late Franco Marchesi's artistic tastes and the hotel displays pieces of art from the family's personal collection.

La Sirenuse
Why La Sirenuse Positano?
  • Most rooms have balconies and a view of the Positano Bay.
  • Lovely pool, gorgeous restaurant and a full service wellness center.
  • Consider a visit during the hotel's bi-yearly fitness/detox retreat, Dolce Vitality.

Sorrento

Oasi Madre della Pace

"Strengthen ties with nature and others in a boundary of peace and serenity" is the ideal behind Oasi Madre della Pace.

The hotel has many unique onsite activities. Guests may participate in harvesting seasonal fruit, or prepare traditonal Sorrento cuisine during a cooking class hosted by the hotel. Holy Mass, morning, and evening prayers are open to guests.

Why Oasi Madre della Pace?
  • Nuns welcome you to the hotel.
  • Enviromentally friendly. Hotel is equipped with solar panels and is energy efficient.
  • Bunnies, peacocks, goats, and other animals on the grounds.

Book a room at La Tonneralla and get access to your own private beach. The hotel has a private lift for its guests. After you take in the waves, enjoy a dinner at La Tonnarella Mare. Their website boasts that the chef "prepares a daily menu inspired by the traditional neapolitan culinary, with particular attention to the flavors of the sea." The restaurant can be reached by boat if you're not a hotel guest.

Why La Tonneralla?
  • Convenient location grants easy access to Positano, Amalfi Ravello and the Islands of the Gulf of Naples.
  • Private beachwith its own Restaurant La Tonnarella Mare.
  • Many rooms have gorgeous panoramic views of the Gulf of Naples
